About NEET Study Tracker

Our Mission

NEET Study Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed specifically for NEET aspirants to help them stay organized, track their preparation progress, and achieve their medical education goals.

What We Offer

  • Complete Syllabus Tracking: Track every chapter of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Study Time Logging: Monitor your daily study hours and maintain consistency
  • Test Score Analytics: Log mock test scores and visualize your progress
  • Question Practice Tracking: Track chapter-wise question practice with graphs
  • Progress Analytics: Comprehensive analytics showing your preparation journey

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Free for all students
  • No advertisements or hidden charges
  • Secure and private - your data is safe
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Regular updates and improvements
