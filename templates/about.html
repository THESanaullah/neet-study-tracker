Why NEET Study Tracker?

1. Complete Syllabus Coverage

Track every single chapter from the official NEET syllabus across all three subjects. Mark your progress with detailed checkboxes for reading NCERT, watching lectures, solving questions, and revisions.

2. Scientific Study Methods

Built-in Pomodoro timer helps you study using the proven 25-minute focus technique, maximizing concentration and retention.

3. Performance Analytics

Log your mock test scores and visualize your performance trends over time. Identify weak areas and track improvements.

4. Revision Management

Never forget to revise important chapters. Our smart reminder system notifies you when chapters need review based on spaced repetition principles.

5. Motivation Through Tracking

See your progress percentage grow daily. Track study streaks and celebrate small wins to stay motivated throughout your journey.
